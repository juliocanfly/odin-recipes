PIZZA!

*** INGREDIENTS ***

- 500 ML OF WARM WATER

- 40 GR OF YEAST

- 1 KG OF ALL-PURPOSE FLOUR

- 20 GR OF SALT

- 25 ML OF OLIVE OIL

- 1 KG OF CHERRY TOMATOES

- 4 CRUSHED GARLIC CLOVES

- OLIVES

- SALT AND PEPPER

- MOZZARELLA CHEESE

- FRESH BASIL LEAVES

*** STEP-BY-STEP ***

1. PUT 500 ML OF WARM (NOT HOT) WATER IN A BOWL.

2. CRUMBLE 40 GR OF YEAST AND DISSOLVE WITH YOUR HANDS.

3. ADD 1 KG OF PLAIN FLOUR, 20 GR OF SALT (WITHOUT LETTING IT TOUCH THE LIQUID SO AS NOT TO RUIN THE YEAST).

4. MIX LITTLE BY LITTLE AND ADD 25 ML OF OLIVE OIL.

5. WHEN A DOUGH FORMS, TRANSFER TO A FLOURED COUNTER AND KNEAD FOR ABOUT 10 MINUTES UNTIL YOU HAVE A SMOOTH, SOFT DOUGH. COVER AND LET IT REST FOR ABOUT AN HOUR, UNTIL IT DOUBLES IN SIZE.

6. MEANWHILE, MAKE THE SAUCE: PUT 1 KG OF WHOLE CHERRY TOMATOES IN A BAKING DISH WITH 4 CRUSHED GARLIC CLOVES, OLIVE OIL, SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE.

7. BAKE IN A HOT OVEN FOR APPROXIMATELY 30/40 MINUTES, UNTIL THEY ARE WELL COOKED AND BURSTING.

8. ONCE READY, MASH WITH A POTATO MASHER AND SET ASIDE.

9. RETURN TO THE RISEN DOUGH BALL, DEGAS AND MAKE TWO BALLS (YOU CAN SEPARATE THEM INTO 3 OR 4 BALLS AS WELL).

10. LET THEM REST UNTIL THEY DOUBLE IN SIZE AGAIN (ABOUT 30 MORE MINUTES).

11. PUT THEM IN A MOLD WITH A LITTLE OIL, STRETCH FROM THE INSIDE OUT, ADD SAUCE, EXTRA OLIVE OIL, MOZZARELLA CHEESE IN BARS, GRATED MOZZARELLA, OR BOTH.

12. BAKE IN A HOT OVEN FOR 10/15 MINUTES, OR UNTIL THE DOUGH IS READY AND BEGINS TO BROWN AROUND THE EDGES.

13. FINISH WITH A FEW FRESH BASIL LEAVES AND ...READY TO ENJOY!
